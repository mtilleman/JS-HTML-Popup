Product Conditions

Box Condition

  • New Factory Sealed (NFS) - Brand new factory sealed in original box.
  • New Sealed Inside (NSI) - Brand new sealed inside packaging in original box, box has been opened.
  • New Open Box (NOB) - New inside original box, box and packaging have been opened.
  • New No Box (NNB) - Does not come in original packaging / box.

Grading Scale

  • A Grade - Item is in perfect condition. Fully fuctional with all accessories. No cosmetic damage.
  • B Grade - Item is in good condition. Fully fuctional with all accessories, may have generic accessores. Has very light cosmetic wear. Specifics noted in product description.
  • C Grade - Fuctional with signs of wear. May not include accessories. Specifics noted in product description.
  • REF - Item has been refurbished. Specifics noted in product description.
  • AS IS - Item being sold as is. May have significant wear or only be good for parts. Please read full product description before purchasing.
